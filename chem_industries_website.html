About Us

Leading Chemical Solutions Provider in Maharashtra

Chem Industries Pvt. Ltd., based in Satara, Maharashtra, is a trusted name in industrial chemical solutions. We specialize in manufacturing and supplying a wide range of high-purity chemicals designed to optimize industrial processes and ensure equipment longevity.

Our product portfolio spans from descaling agents that remove hard mineral deposits to specialized boiler and cooling tower treatment chemicals — all formulated to meet the stringent demands of modern industry.

With a commitment to quality, safety, and environmental responsibility, we serve clients across power plants, manufacturing facilities, textile industries, food processing units, and more across India.
