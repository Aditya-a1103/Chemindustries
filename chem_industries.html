Industrial Chemical Products

Descaling Chemical

Highly effective descaling agents formulated to dissolve mineral deposits, rust, and scale from heat exchangers, pipelines, boilers, and industrial equipment. Restores operational efficiency and extends equipment lifespan with minimal downtime.

Maintenance
🔥

Boiler Chemicals

Specialised chemical treatments for steam boilers including oxygen scavengers, scale and corrosion inhibitors, and pH stabilisers. Designed to prevent internal corrosion, control scale build-up, and ensure safe, efficient boiler operation.

Boiler Treatment
🌊

Cooling Tower Chemicals

Complete cooling water treatment programme including corrosion inhibitors, scale preventatives, biocides, and dispersants. Maintains water quality, prevents microbial growth, and protects cooling tower infrastructure for peak thermal performance.

Water Treatment
🧫

Morpholine

Industrial-grade Morpholine used as a corrosion inhibitor and pH-regulating agent in steam boiler and condensate systems. Also widely used as a solvent and intermediate in the synthesis of pharmaceuticals, agrochemicals, and rubber chemicals.

Chemical Compound
🧪

Nitric Acid

High-purity Nitric Acid for industrial, metal treatment, and chemical synthesis applications. Used in the production of fertilisers, explosives, and in metal pickling, cleaning of stainless steel, and passivation processes.

Inorganic Acid
🌿

Phenolin (Phenol)

Industrial Phenolin / Phenol used as a disinfectant, antiseptic, and key feedstock in producing resins, nylon, epoxy, pharmaceuticals, and detergents. Widely applied in wood treatment and as an industrial cleaning agent.

Organic Compound
⚡

Acid Chemicals

A range of industrial-grade acids including Hydrochloric Acid, Sulphuric Acid, and speciality acid blends used in metal processing, pickling, pH control, water treatment, and general industrial cleaning applications.
